Nigeria

Nigeria, officially known as the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country located in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa and the seventh most populous in the world, with over 200 million people. Nigeria is known for its rich cultural diversity, with more than 250 ethnic groups and over 500 languages spoken. The largest ethnic groups are the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo.
