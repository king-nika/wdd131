Site Plan Document

Site Name: Budget Wise

  • This name represents a budget tracker that is used to make wise decisions

Site Purpose: To track and manage finances

  • Budget Wise is designed to help individuals manage their finances effectively by tracking income and expenses in an intuitive and organized way. The platform provides users with real-time insights into their spending habits, allowing them to make informed financial decisions. With interactive charts, categorized transactions, and a user-friendly interface, BudgetWise aims to simplify budgeting and promote financial stability.

Scenario:

  • How can I track my daily expenses and see where my money is going?
  • Can I view a summary of my monthly budget and spending trends?
